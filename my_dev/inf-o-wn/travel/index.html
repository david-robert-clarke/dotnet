Travel Tips

International and Domestic Trips

  1. Tip (Currency): Order your currency at least 1 week in advance. Order at least AU$100 per day of the trip.

    Reason: Better rates available by booking in advance. All establishments take cash, but won't necessarily take credit cards.
    • Travelex
    • OzForex Online ID: davidrobertclarke


  2. Tip (Tours): Book a tour for the first day only. Book remaining trips at tourist information centre at destination.

    Reason: Cheaper rates for subsequent trips. Better choice available.

  3. Tip (Tours): Do not book a scenic flight.

    Reason: Not subject to the elements. Rushed. Do not get a feel for the scale. Requires photographing through window, which impair picture.

  4. Tip (Restaurant): Before holiday begins search for recommended restaurants that serve local dishes.

    Reason: When at the destination no one has a clue where the best local dining places are.
