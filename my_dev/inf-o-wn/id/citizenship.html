Attaching Documents

  1. Documents must be scanned and converted to .JPG format. You must avoid using spaces ' '.
  2. Copies of original documents must be certified by a person from the designated list of occupations for all applications including those lodged overseas.
  3. If you attend a citizenship appointment you must bring your original documents with you.
