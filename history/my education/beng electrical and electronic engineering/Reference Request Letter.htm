Reference Request

The applicant who gives you this letter is applying for admission to the programme of study which they have indicated below. We understand from the applicant that you have agreed to act as a referee and hope that they have been able to consult you about this application. We therefore ask you to provide overleaf your opinion on the applicant's suitability to study for the degree in question.

Normally a good honours degree (or an equivalent qualification, whether British or overseas) would be required for admission to a higher degree programme. For research degrees, the University must be satisfied as to the suitability of the subject proposed for the thesis and the applicant's ability to carry out a piece of work to the required standard. The thesis will be expected to offer evidence of original work and show competence in the relevant methods of research, clear presentation of material and independent judgment.

Taught programmes of study are generally of 12 months' full-time or 24 months' part-time duration, with assessment by examination and thesis. Applicants are expected to demonstrate that they can present material clearly and exercise independent judgment.

As the application cannot be considered until we have received your reference, its early return would be appreciated. Please return the reference directly to the University of Birmingham.

We take this opportunity to thank you in advance for assisting the applicant. Whilst we are not normally able to acknowledge references, we should be happy to do so if asked.
