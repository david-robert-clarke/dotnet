Summary

David is a versatile Technical Test Consultant with 10 Years experience spanning roles in the Logistics, Government, Education, Finance, Legal, Insurance, Construction, Media and Transport industry sectors. He has full systems and test development life cycle experience, including use of Agile & V-Model test methodologies. David is a Certified Agile Tester (iSQI CAT). He is ISEB-qualified to Foundation level and has gained Certified Software Test Professional (CSTP) Test Management certification in Australia. David is also a Certified Professional Consultant in several of the HP testing products and can be considered an expert in automated testing and automation frameworks. David's commitment to success and determination to provide output of a consistently high standard will ensure he is a valuable asset to any project team.
